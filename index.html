Got Questions?

Can I bring my family with me?
Yes! The §81a procedure allows for family reunification. We handle the paperwork for your spouse and children simultaneously so you can arrive in Germany together.
Do I need to speak fluent German?
For IT roles, English is usually sufficient, but A1/A2 German is recommended. For Gastronomy, B1 German is mandatory. We provide language training resources if needed.
What happens if my visa is rejected?
We only take on candidates we are 99% sure will pass. If a rejection occurs due to authority error, we handle the legal appeal. If it's a structural rejection, we have a transparent refund policy for the second installment.
Is the 20,000€ fee negotiable?
This is a premium, all-inclusive service that includes legal fees, housing setup, and direct hiring. We do not negotiate the fee, as it ensures the quality and speed of the Turbo-Track process.
